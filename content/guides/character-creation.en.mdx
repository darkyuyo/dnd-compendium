# Character creation

Follow these steps to build a level-1 character. Links point to compendium sections.

## 1. Choose a species

Browse [Species](/en/species) (human, elf, dwarf, …) and note traits, size, and speed.

## 2. Choose a background

[Backgrounds](/en/backgrounds) grant suggested ability score increases, proficiencies, gear, and often an **origin feat**.

## 3. Choose a class

Review [Classes](/en/classes): hit die, proficiencies, and 1st-level features.

## 4. Determine ability scores

Use your table's method (point buy, standard array, or rolling). Apply background bonuses and record modifiers.

## 5. Record combat stats and gear

- Armor Class, hit points, initiative, and speed.
- Weapons from [Weapons](/en/weapons) and items from [Equipment](/en/equipment).

## 6. Spells (if any)

If your class casts spells, pick cantrips and 1st-level spells from [Spells](/en/spells).

## 7. Feats and finishing details

Check [Feats](/en/feats), write personality, ideal, and bonds. Ready to play!

For session flow, see [How to play](/en/how-to-play).
